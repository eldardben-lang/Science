From food to cell — the energy pathway
You eat food → Digestion breaks it down → Absorbed by blood → Delivered to cells → Energy released! ⚡
Mechanical digestion
Physical breaking — chewing, stomach churning. Makes food smaller so enzymes can work more efficiently.
Chemical digestion
Enzymes and acids break food into tiny molecules. Happens in the mouth, stomach and small intestine.
⚡
Cellular respiration!Once glucose reaches cells, it combines with oxygen to release energy — this powers everything you do!
